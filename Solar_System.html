CONTROLS:
Press 'B' to control the astronaut.
While in B-mode, you can move the astronaut with WASD
and extract the sword pressing 'G' and attack with 'J'.
In order to sheathe the sword, press 'H'.
Press 'P' and then select a number from 0 to 9 to see the planets.
